Kumagaya

Kumagaya LLC. is a Japanese streetwear brand based in Chicago. Co-Founder Astin created this brand while walking around the streets of Chicago comparing big brand-name stores to small streetwear boutiques. Upon realizing the difference in quality, and the social conflict of Japanese culture being used inaccurately, he decided to create his brand to represent authentic Japanese art and culture through clothes. Astin is a native from Japan and from Kumagaya which means Bear Valley; hence the bear logo.
