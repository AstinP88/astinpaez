Career

Since 2016, Astin has been working as an artist manager for two electronic music groups for whom he's handled their contracts (both performance production and music publishing), organized itineraries, and strategized marketing procedures to increase their fanbase.

Kolla is an EDM duo based out of the north suburbs of Chicago. Julian and Casey, were freshman in High School when their journey first began. 2017-2019 were fun filled years that consisted of shows with Saint Wknd, Jerry Folk, Slushii, Win and Woo, Whethan, Ookay, Gryffin, and JawBreaker festival. The Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago hosted Kolla for an event called 21 Minus which highlighted the up and coming artists in Chicago who are under 21years of age. As 2020 approached, Julian, now known as Tazu has become a solo act, and is focusing on dubstep/house.
